About

Shoelace is a totally cool OAuth2 provider that takes all the stupid hard work out of it. The sorry state of Python OAuth2 providers really doesn't help the fact that setting one up is a monumental pain in the ass. The authors are remiss to use the phrase batteries included, but it's kinda fitting.
